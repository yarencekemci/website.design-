ABOUT US

Information about our restaurant

We have restaurants in many central points. The service is open between 8.00 - 23.00 7 days a week.

Many kinds of healthy and hygienic meals are available in our restaurant. Our venue has an open and closed area.

We meet at our restaurant for you to taste the delicious dishes of our chefs.

As for dinner; The menu has taken a bit of flavor from all over the world. Nachos, Burritos from Mexico, Buffalo Chicken Wings from California, steak steak with a French classic Cafe de Paris sauce, Italian-style thin crust pizzas and fabulous Cheese Cakes.

Restaurant, which is the second address of everyone from seven to seventy, was awarded the most visited Cafe award by Boğaziçi University services twice.
